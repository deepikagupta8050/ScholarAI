\# 🎓 ScholarAI



AI Powered Research Intelligence Platform



\## 🚀 Overview



ScholarAI is an AI-powered platform that helps students, researchers, and academicians streamline the research process through intelligent paper analysis, literature reviews, citation generation, and research gap identification.



\## ✨ Features



\- Research Paper Analysis

\- AI Chat with Research Papers

\- Literature Review Generation

\- Research Gap Detection

\- Citation Generation

\- Research Roadmap Creation

\- Semantic Research Search



\## 🛠️ Tech Stack



\### Frontend

\- Next.js

\- TypeScript

\- Tailwind CSS



\### Backend

\- FastAPI

\- Python



\### AI \& ML

\- LangChain

\- ChromaDB

\- Sentence Transformers
